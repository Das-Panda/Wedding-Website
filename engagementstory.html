No one knows Kenneth and I have actually two engagement stories! The first time he proposed was at the end of August 2008 over the phone while I was doing research in the US Virgin Islands. Of course I said yes, but we kept it to ourselves mainly because I didn't want to scare family members into thinking I was somehow not going to finish college just because I was engaged. We kept it between ourselves for quite some time and when I returned from the USVI I had placed my heart ring on my left hand – symbolizing I had made my choice in terms of who I wanted to spend the rest of my life with and who I wanted to have to buy old people diapers for when he gets old.

Keeping the promise I made to my dad on finishing school before marriage in my mind, I secretly wanted a traditional proposal from Kenneth IN PERSON preferably when we were both on the continental United States! Kenneth, being the romantic guy he is, also wanted to do something special for me. The morning before graduating from college, we both wanted to spend some time together by going to breakfast on Coronado Island. I knew something was up and I think he knew I knew, when he pulled and parked in front of the beach by the Hotel Del Coronado. He said let's take a walk. My heart was racing and pounding against my chest. The man hates sand, and here we go off walking together on the morning beach. Fresh. No footprints made by others yet. He bent down by a large boulder and I stopped breathing I think…He mentioned something about tying his shoe and my vision blurred I think. I was so excited. He turned around as he was on one knee and I'm sure what he said was lovely – my heart and mind and soul were all jumping at once so I have no idea what he said…I do remember him saying he wanted to spend the rest of forever with me….Of course I reconfirmed with him the second most important decision of my life (my decision to follow Christ was the first) in the affirmative, yes, yes, yes!
